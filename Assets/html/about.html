Who we are!

History

Since our establishment on December 1, 2007, nestled in the heart of Mukkola, Vizhinjam, our dental clinic has become a cornerstone of oral health for the past 16 years. Situated conveniently close to the picturesque destination of Kovalam, we've had the privilege of serving a diverse clientele, including tourists from various parts of the world.
Our enduring presence has allowed us to build lasting relationships within the Vizhinjam locality, fostering trust and community bonds. Over the years, we have become a trusted destination not only for locals but also for individuals from different parts of Trivandrum and neighboring districts seeking quality dental care. We take pride in our role as a beacon of oral health, offering personalized and comprehensive services to all those who walk through our doors.

ISO Certification

We take pride in our commitment to quality, as evidenced by our ISO 9001:2015 certification. This certification underscores our dedication to providing high standards of dental care and ensuring the satisfaction and well-being of all our patients.

Vision

Elegance Dental Clinic and Implant Centre envisions a pioneering role in oral healthcare, delivering cutting-edge, personalized solutions with a touch of elegance. We strive to be synonymous with excellence, blending innovation, compassion, and affordability for a lasting impact on our patients' well-being and confidence.

Mission

Our mission is to provide unparalleled dental care through clinical expertise, advanced technology, and patient-centric service. We create a welcoming environment where individuals feel valued on their path to optimal oral health. Elegance Dental Clinic and Implant Centre aim to empower the community with knowledge and tools for enduring smiles, offering refined care that combines sophistication with affordability and accessibility.

Dr. Mohamed Sajid BDS

Founder & Chief Dental Surgeon

Dr. Mohamed Sajid BDS , an accomplished general practitioner, stands as the visionary force behind Elegance Dental Care and Implant Centre. With a deep-rooted commitment to dental excellence, Dr. Sajid initiated this center to bring forth a unique blend of expertise and compassionate care. His keen interest in implantology reflects a dedication to staying at the forefront of dental advancements, ensuring that patients receive state-of-the-art treatments.
Under the able leadership of Dr. Mohamed Sajid, Elegance Dental Care and Implant Centre embody the essence of elegance in both its name and practice. Driven by a passion for precision and patient satisfaction, Dr. Sajid leads a team of skilled associates, collectively working to create a dental experience that resonates with sophistication and quality care.
